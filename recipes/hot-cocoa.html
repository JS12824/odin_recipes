The Best Hot Cocoa

Description of Deliciousness

This is my wife's recipe for hot cocoa. Truly, there is no better drink to have after shoveling that snow covered driveway

Ingredients

  • One favorite mug full of hot milk
  • Two Hershey's Kisses
  • All the marshmallows you could ever want
  • Optional:Chocolate powder, shavings or syrup

Steps

  1. Warm the milk in a pan on the stove or simply in the mug with the microwave
  2. When milk is steaming drop in the two Hershey's Kisses
  3. Stir until Kisses melt
  4. Add desired amount of marshmallows
  5. Optional: Sprinkle chocolate dust or shavings on the marshmallows. Syrup is a great option as well
