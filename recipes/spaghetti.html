Spaghetti

Description of Deliciousness

A simple and quick spaghetti recipe.

Ingredients

  • One jar of your favorite sauce
  • Half box of spaghetti noodles

Steps

  1. Bring a pot of water to boil on the stove
  2. Add noodles to water and cook for about ten-15 minutes. Noodles will be soft and floppy when done
  3. Strain noodles in sink to remove the water
  4. Stir in sauce
  5. Optional: Brown a pound of ground beef and mix in to add a protein element
