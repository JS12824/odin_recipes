Grilled Cheese

Description of Deliciousness

This delicious and decadent sandwich will have you coming back for more. Creamy cheese nestled between two perfectly toasted slices of bread seems so simple. And it is!

Ingredients

  • Two slices of your favorite bread. We recommend a tasty sourdough.
  • Two slices of your favorite cheese. Gouda is always a classy choice.
  • A few dabs of butter.
  • Optional:Bacon

Steps

  1. Preheat skillet on stove
  2. Butter one side of each slice of bread
  3. Place both slices of bread in the pan butter side down
  4. Once the bread has had some time to heat up, place 1 or 2 slices of cheese on one slice of bread
  5. Take the uncheesed slice of bread and flip over on to the cheesed slice.
  6. Take the uncheesed slice of bread and flip over on to the cheesed slice.
